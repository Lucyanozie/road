Hot Milk Cake

Hot Milk Cake is a light, fluffy, and moist cake that's perfect for any occasion. It’s quick to make and doesn’t require any fancy ingredients, but the result is simply delicious!

Ingredients

        1 cup granulated sugar
        1/2 cup whole milk 1
        1/2 cup unsalted butter
        2 large eggs
        1 tsp vanilla extract
        1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 2
		1 tsp baking powder
        1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8x8-inch baking pan or line it with parchment paper.

Step 2: In a small saucepan, combine the milk and butter. Heat over medium heat until the butter is melted and the mixture is hot. Remove from heat and set aside to cool slightly.

Step 3: In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs and sugar with an electric mixer on medium-high speed for 2-3 minutes until light and fluffy.

Step 4: Gradually add the vanilla extract, flour 3, baking powder, and salt. Mix until just combined.

Step 5: Slowly add the warm milk-butter mixture into the batter, stirring gently until smooth.

Step 6: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 7: Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

For a richer flavor, use heavy cream instead of whole milk. For extra fluffiness, add a tablespoon of sour cream to the batter.
